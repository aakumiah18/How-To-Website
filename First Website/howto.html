Steps 2 Baking a Dope Cake


Baking a cake is totally difficult, unless you have been taught the right way. Reading below, and in no time you'll know how to make a cake as dope as this:



Steps:

  1. First you need to get your materials and ingredients. For this recipe you will need:
    • an oven
    • a greased pan
    • cream
    • 1 cup of unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 cup of sugar
    • 5 eggs
    • 2 tea spoons of vanilla extract
    • 2 cups of cake flour
    • a large mixing bowl

  2. Next, preheat the oven to 325ÂºF

  3. After that, put the cream, butter, and sugar into a bowl mix until it becomes a creamy consistancy

  4. When you've finished mixing, add the eggs, vanilla extract, and cake flour and mix again

  5. Then, pour the batter into the greased pan, making sure to spread evenly

  6. Now, place in the oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes

  7. Finally, take it out, let it cool, and enjoy or prepare to decorate.
